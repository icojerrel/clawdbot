## ğŸ‡³ğŸ‡± DUTCH LEGAL COMPLIANCE

### Age Verification Requirements
**Netherlands Alcohol Law:**
- Legal drinking age: **18 years**
- ID verification required for purchases
- No sales to visibly intoxicated persons
- No advertising targeting minors

### Advertising Standards
**Per Nederlandse Reclame Code (NRC):**
1. No appeals to minors
2. No excessive consumption promotion
3. No association with dangerous activities
4. Responsible drinking messages required
5. No health benefit claims

### Platform Requirements
**Instagram/Facebook (Meta):**
- Age-gate content for 18+
- Include responsible drinking warnings
- Comply with alcohol advertising policies

**TikTok:**
- Mark content as "Adult Content"
- Include age disclaimers
- Follow Community Guidelines on alcohol
